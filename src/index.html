About Us

Welcome to our Task Board, a web-based application developed as a school project using PHP. Our task board allows users to create and manage tasks, assign them to team members, and track progress. With a user-friendly interface and powerful functionality, it's the perfect tool for keeping your team organized and on track. Whether you're a student working on a group project or a professional managing a team, our task board has everything you need to stay organized and productive. We hope you enjoy using it!
